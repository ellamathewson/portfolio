Boogie Worms

Personal JavaScript Project

I starting creating this game as my summer project of 2018. Using the cursor, the player needs to catch the "boogie worms" on the screen. Every five rounds, an element of play changes to increase the difficulty, such as size of the light, speed of the boogie worms, time limit, etc.

This is one of my first independent projects I made after my first year of college. There is a lot I want to change and add to it, such as a better UI and implementing React.js and accounts to have scoreboards to it. I am continuing to work on it when I find time throughout the school year.
