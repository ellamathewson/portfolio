Historic Campus

RIT Research Project

I was the UI lead on this team project, and I worked to create a website hosting the information and the Unity build. The purpose of the website is to showcase our project of what RIT's campus used to look like when it was located downtown. By having it as a web build the final product is more accessible since the main product was an AR app for Android and iOS. Due to this, there are a few hiccups with the Unity build, but all the information we wanted to highlight is there.
