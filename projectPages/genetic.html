Genetic Algorithms

Creative Art Final Project

For the final project in my Interactive Media class we researched a topic and created a website highlighting the key points of what technological device we researched. This website highlights the key points of the benefits of wearable technologies.

Although there is not a lot of complex functionality, the design of the website is what I'm most proud of and why it's on my portfolio. I highlighed visual elements to make the website an enjoyable experience while also being factual and informative.
