Exercise for my class in Current Topics in Interactive Development. The goal for this was to make our own custom target for the camera to recognize and display the scene on top of. Using Vueforia Developer Portal I uploaded an image of Bigfoot as the target (Source). In Unity I used models to create a simple camping scene on top of the target so when the camera sees the target, the scene is displayed.
